This is a placeholder for your logo.png file.

To add your logo:
1. Place your logo.png file in the /public/ folder
2. Make sure it's named exactly "logo.png"
3. Recommended size: 512x512 pixels or smaller
4. The logo will automatically appear in all locations where the SevaNet logo is displayed

Current logo locations:
- Homepage header and footer
- Login and registration pages
- Dashboard sidebar
- All navigation areas

The app will automatically fall back to the Building2 icon if logo.png is not found.
